For this project, I will investigate whether used electric vehicle (EV) listings are priced higher than comparable gas-powered vehicle listings in the U.S. This question matters because EV adoption is increasing, and understanding pricing differences can provide insight into market trends and affordability. I will test the hypothesis that EV listings have a higher median price than gas vehicle listings using a permutation test, and I will estimate uncertainty using bootstrapping. The dataset will come from the Craigslist Used Cars dataset available on Kaggle (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/austinreese/craigslist-carstrucks-data
), which includes detailed information such as price, fuel type, mileage, and location for each listing.
